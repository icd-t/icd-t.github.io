Review
(Spoiler Free)

The game was made on Roblox, which might be a turn off for some people. The graphics and audience might not be what you are looking for, but it is still very fun.

What makes this game special is the combination of large maps and player counts with fast paced techincal movement and in-depth weapon customization. The game has a long and winding skill curve, and a huge amount of depth.

Its problems begin to show with the same problems as all FPS games. 60% of the players use the same ~15 or so meta weapons. Despite this, there isn't too much toxisity, and I haven't seen a single cheater in several months.
