Cool Independent Sites

These are some cool websites that I've stumbled across over the years. Anything from a random blog to a physics game. I'll try to limit them to only ones worth your time.

neal.fun Everyone knows this website. Neal has been making fun little interactive games for decades now, and if you haven't been to his site, go do so right now.

xkcd Again, if you don't know who xkcd is, you haven't been on the internet. Hilarious daily nerdy web comics.

NeoCities A place to find other places. Reviving the old web.

Wiby A search engine specifically for old websites, or anything that dosen't use JS. Find some really old stuff here.

Eric Murphy He is only here because I am legally obligated to put him here. (No but seriously hes pretty cool).

Ascii Art Archive An archive of Ascii and Text art. This is where I got the Ascii art of the cat on my home page.

OSINT Geolocation Challenges Challenges made by an individual to test your geolocation skills. I've completed a few of them, and its pretty fun.

Wired Sound (EPILEPSY WARNING) A very cool looking personal website I found.

Oddity Commoddity Another very cool retro themed website! Lots of buttons and things to poke around in!
