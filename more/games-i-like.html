Phantom Forces

Free Shooter Roblox





Love it or hate it, but Roblox is here to stay. And despite the juvinile art style, some of the games are genuinly amazing. One of these games is Phantom Forces, an arcade COD style movement shooter. It has a near endless selection of guns and attachments, and the movement is so fluid and satisfying. I've put a good thousand hours into this game, and think it deserves more recognition.



Geoguessr

$36 / Year Geography Geoguessr





Despite it's high price (there is a free alternative), this game is insanly fun. If you're a nerd like me, this game is something else. The premise is that you are put in a randomly slected google maps street view location, and you have to find you where you are in a certain amount of time.
