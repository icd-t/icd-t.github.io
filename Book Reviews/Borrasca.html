Review
(Spoiler Free)

Borrasca is an incredible horror short thriller about teenagers discovering the grim and disgusting history of their town in the Ozarks. Split into 4 short parts, the whole story can be read in just an afternoon.

Through out the story, you are slowly drip-fed information that makes the twist all the more sickening. The author leaves you thinking about the characters and the town, and then lets you come to the realization that the true horror of this story is that it could really happen.

Some of the problems with the story include odd dialogue between some of the characters, however this is only a minor flaw.
